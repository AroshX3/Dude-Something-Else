Site for the Students

You're in the Right Place

This site is built exclusively for our class — to help you keep track of schoolwork, notices, class tests, and more. It’s simple, clean, and student-powered.

Why Are You Here?

You're here because someone (maybe me) shared this early access site with you — but it's not just for fun. This is a welcome page for something much bigger. A platform built specifically for the students of our class. Designed to help, inform, and make school life just a little more organized (and maybe less boring). You're part of the early squad — congrats on being here first.

What's the Purpose?

The main goal of this project is to give our class something we’ve never had — our own site for homework updates, class tests, CT schedules, exam notices, and anything school-related. No more hunting down notes or asking “when’s the test again?” in 10 different group chats. Everything will be in one place. Simple. Clean. Useful.

Still Curious?

Right now, this is just the welcome page — a preview of what’s coming soon. The real site is still under construction (coded from scratch, no templates). But once it’s ready, it’ll be your go-to hub for staying updated. Homework? Posted. Notices? Updated. Exams? Listed. No more missing out. So if you're curious… stay tuned. You’re gonna love what’s coming.
